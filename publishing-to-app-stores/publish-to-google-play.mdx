# Publish to Google Play  

Publishing React Native apps to Play Store follows the same process across the industry. So our apps are being published exactly as a regular React Native app. This means that you can follow the official React Native docs on [how to publish the app to the Google Play Store](https://reactnative.dev/docs/signed-apk-android).
